Welcome to TAS Employee 360-Feedback Survey

Purpose

A 360-Feedback Assessment is a multi-source assessmement in which feedback from an employee's subordinates, colleagues, and supervisor(s), as well as a self-evaluation by the employee themselves is gathered. This tool provides and individual a full understanding of his/her strengths and points of improvement. By assessing our employees on a semi-annual basis, we strive to ensure our team has current feedback to form both short and long term goals. It is the resposibility of the team member to give honest and constructive feedback to improve the quality for all employees.

Scale


Insufficient

Performance was consistently below expectations, and/or reasonable progress toward critical goals was not made. Significant improvement is needed.

Insufficient


Inconsistent

Performance did not properly meet expectations and/or one of the most critical goals was not met.

Inconsistent


Successful

Performance met expectations in terms of quality of work, and efficiency and timeliness. The most critial annual goals were met.

Successful


Excellent

Performance exceeded expectations. All goals, objectives and tangets were achieved above the established standards.

Excellent


Outstanding

Extraordinary level of achievement and commitment in terms of quality and time, technical skills and knowledge, ingenuity, creativity, and initative.

Outstanding
